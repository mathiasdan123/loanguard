# 123 Main Street Office Building - Compliance Checklist

Borrower: Sample Borrower LLC

## Financial Reporting

- [ ] **Quarterly Financial Statements**
  - Send your quarterly financial statements to the lender within 45 days after each quarter ends
  - ⏰ 45 days after quarter end

- [ ] **Annual Audited Financials**
  - Have a CPA audit your financials and send the report within 120 days after year end
  - ⏰ 120 days after fiscal year end

- [ ] **Monthly Rent Roll**
  - Send a current rent roll showing all tenants, lease terms, and rental rates by the 15th of each month
  - ⏰ By the 15th of each month

- [ ] **Annual Operating Budget**
  - Submit next year's operating budget for lender approval by November 15th each year
  - ⏰ November 15 annually

## Covenant Compliance

- [ ] **DSCR Covenant**
  - Your property's net operating income divided by your loan payments must be at least 1.25x
  - ⏰ Tested quarterly

## Insurance

- [ ] **Property Insurance**
  - Keep your property insured for full replacement cost. Send proof to lender before each renewal.
  - ⏰ 30 days before policy expiration

## Reserve Funding

- [ ] **Replacement Reserve Deposits**
  - Deposit $2,500 monthly into your replacement reserve account
  - ⏰ Monthly with mortgage payment

## Leasing

- [ ] **Major Lease Approval**
  - Get lender approval before signing any lease over 10,000 SF or with a tenant getting more than 3 months free rent
  - ⏰ Prior to lease execution
